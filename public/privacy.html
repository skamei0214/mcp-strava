Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Athlete Coach for Claude ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal project that connects your Strava training data to AI assistants including Claude and ChatGPT. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What data we collect

When you connect your Strava account, we store:

  • Your Strava athlete ID
  • Your Strava access token and refresh token (used to fetch your activity data)
  • Your activity data as returned by the Strava API, including: activity names, descriptions, dates, distances, times, heart rate, pace, and lap data

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information beyond what is described above.

How we use your data

Your Strava data is fetched on demand and returned directly to the AI assistant you are using (Claude or ChatGPT) for the purpose of training analysis and coaching. We do not use your data for any other purpose, including advertising, analytics, or sale to third parties.

How we store your data

Tokens and activity data are stored in a secured database on a private server. Activity data is fetched fresh from Strava each time it is requested and is not persistently cached. Tokens are stored only as long as your account remains connected.

Strava data usage

This app accesses your Strava data through the official Strava API in accordance with the Strava API Agreement. Strava may monitor and collect certain usage data related to your use of Strava's API. Your use of Strava data through this app is also subject to Strava's Privacy Policy.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties, except as required to provide the service (i.e., passing your activity data to the AI assistant you have chosen to use in your own session).

Your rights

You can disconnect your Strava account at any time by revoking access in your Strava app settings. You can also request deletion of all stored data by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

GDPR and UK GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
