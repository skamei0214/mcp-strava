Setup guide

Connect in 3 steps

ℹ️  Initial setup must be done on claude.ai in a desktop browser. Once connected, Claude can access your Strava from any device — including the mobile app.
1

Create your own Strava API app

This connector uses your own Strava API credentials, so your data stays under your control and is never shared with other users.

Go to strava.com/settings/api and create a new application. The only field that actually matters is Authorization Callback Domain — everything else is for Strava's records and can be anything.

After creating the app, keep this tab open — you'll need your Client ID and Client Secret in Step 3.

2

Add the connector to Claude

On claude.ai, go to Profile → Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector and fill in:

Name
Strava Connector (or anything you like)
URL
https://187-77-203-66.sslip.io/mcp

If the button shows Configure instead of Connect, tap the ellipsis, Disconnect, and then Connect again to reset the connection.

3

Connect your Strava account

In the popup, paste your Client ID and Client Secret from Step 1, then click Connect.

You'll be taken to Strava to authorize, then shown a confirmation screen. Confirm it's your account — and you're done. Claude can now access your Strava data from any device.

If the popup didn't appear in Step 2, go to Settings → Connectors, click the connector, then click Reconnect to trigger it.

🔒

Your data stays yours

This connector uses your own Strava API credentials — not a shared app. Your activities are fetched directly from Strava's API and passed to Claude. Nothing is stored beyond what's needed to keep your account connected. Privacy policy →
